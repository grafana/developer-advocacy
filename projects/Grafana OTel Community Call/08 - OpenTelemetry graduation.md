## Talking points

> Enumerate talking points for the show. It's better to keep these as bullet points to encourage a more casual, rather than scripted, conversation.

- Intro
	- *Hello and welcome to Grafana OTel Community Call. I'm `<name>`, a `<position>` at Grafana Labs and today, we're going to talk about OpenTelemetry's graduation from the CNCF.*
- Announcements
- Introduce guests
	- Ted Young
	- Marylia Gutierrez
	- Tiffany Hrabusa
- [ ] What does CNCF graduation actually mean?
	- The CNCF maturity levels (Sandbox / Incubating / Graduated) and what each signals to adopters
	- Criteria OpenTelemetry had to meet: adoption, governance, security audits, diversity of contributors
	- How long did this take, and what was the journey like?
- [ ] Why this matters to users
	- What changes for end users now that OTel is graduated?
	- Signals of long-term stability for enterprises betting on OTel
	- How does graduation compare to component stability (signals, SDKs, semconv)?
- [ ] The road here
	- Key milestones in the project's history
	- What were the hardest parts of getting to graduation?
	- Surprising moments along the way
- [ ] The state of the signals
	- Traces, metrics, logs — where is each today?
	- Profiling and other emerging signals
	- Semantic conventions progress
- [ ] What's next after graduation?
	- Priorities for the next year
	- Areas that still need contributors
	- How can the community help?
- [ ] The human side
	- Each guest's OTel story: how did you get involved?
	- Favorite moment in the project
	- Advice for someone wanting to contribute today
