## Summary

In this video, the speaker shares their experience of attempting to create a candle monitoring system to track PM particles released when a candle is extinguished and to check if a candle is still burning while away from home. They discuss the potential safety hazards of the project, including a fire incident caused by using a wooden lid during testing. The conversation highlights the lessons learned about the dangers of working with fire and the importance of safety precautions, as well as the challenges faced during the project.
