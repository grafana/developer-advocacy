## Description

Grafana Assistant is built with security and control at its core, giving organizations full ownership over how and when it's used.

## Summary

In this video, the presenter discusses the opt-in nature of a new feature related to the Grafana assistant, emphasizing that it is not enabled by default. The speaker highlights the importance of organizational policies regarding data usage, noting that data is only sent to the language model when a user interacts with the assistant, and assures that no data is used for training purposes. Additionally, the video mentions that administrators have the ability to enable or disable this feature per stack, ensuring that access to data sources is strictly controlled.

Lastly, this is **opt-in**. These features are not enabled by default, which gives you the opportunity to discuss with your organization and determine your policies regarding this.

For **data usage**, data is only sent to the LLM when a user actively interacts with the assistant. Even then, no data is used for training. 

Regarding **control**, admins can enable or disable this feature per stack, and everything is managed through our backend. Therefore, if I do not have access to a particular data source, then neither does the Grafana assistant.
