## Summary

The video discusses the challenges organizations face with rising observability costs that outpace the insights gained from it. It emphasizes the need for clear visibility and effective cost management as workloads scale, leading to excessive telemetry data that may not deliver value. Grafana Cloud is presented as a solution, offering real-time cost tracking across its suite of products, adaptive telemetry for optimizing data usage, and tools for monitoring and controlling spending. Key features include a cost management dashboard, cost attribution for accountability, and the ability to maintain critical signals while reducing unnecessary data. This allows teams to manage their observability costs effectively and confidently scale their operations.

If your observability bill is growing faster than the insights it delivers, you are not alone. As your workloads scale, telemetry is exploding, and suddenly your team is paying for data that adds very little value. This means costs become harder to justify, and your confidence in your observability investment starts to become a concern.

What your team needs is **clear visibility** and a way to control spending without compromising coverage of your services and infrastructure. **Grafana Cloud** provides both.

With **cost management**, your team gets a real-time view of spending across logs, metrics, traces, profiles, and every product offering in the Grafana Cloud suite. You can track usage as it happens, monitor contract burn, and see exactly what's driving cost increases and where they're coming from. When something changes, you can quickly spot emerging cost trends. When you need full transparency, invoices are available with clear line-item detail, so there are no surprises for the FinOps team.

Once your team understands where the budget is going, Grafana Cloud gives you the tools to act on it. **Adaptive Telemetry** automatically analyzes how your telemetry is actually being used—whether that's in dashboards, alerts, or queries—to determine what's valuable and what's just noise. It then recommends where you can aggregate, sample, or drop low-value telemetry, so you reduce spending without losing signal quality.

Together, these capabilities unlock even more control:

- Start with the **cost management dashboard** to identify the parts of your environment generating the most spend.
- Use **cost attribution** to understand which teams, services, or workloads are responsible.
- Set alerts so your team knows the moment costs are trending in the wrong direction.

Then, apply **Adaptive Telemetry** to optimize the data driving that spend. Critical signals can be marked as exempt, so they're always preserved. From there, you can monitor the impact over time and fine-tune as your environment evolves.

With Grafana Cloud, your team gets full visibility into observability costs, automated tools to control them, and the confidence to scale without losing control of your budget.
